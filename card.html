Octopus

Color and size of Octopus is determined by their environment. Those that live in colder water will be much larger than those that live in tropical (warm) water.

  • Scientific Name: Octopoda
  • Average Length: 16 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years
  • Habitat: Coastal waters

All octopuses have head, called mantle, surrounded with 8 arms, called tentacles. All vital organs are located in their head,they have 3 hearts and their blood is blue in color.
